01 · The Companion

Built for rigorous preparation

Legacy is a hybrid Android application (Apache Cordova) delivering a complete study ecosystem: a searchable Chemistry compendium, an exhaustive Physics formulary & concept codex (Egyptian HTML), two quiz engines, and real‑time performance analytics.

All reference material lives inside the app. The Physics compendium — styled as a royal parchment codex — covers mechanics, thermo, electromagnetism, optics, modern physics, graph intuition, and common traps. Access it directly from the navigation bar.
